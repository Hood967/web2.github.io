Movember

Welcome to the Order of Mo

The Order of Mo is the world’s least secret society. Because we want everyone to know we stand for healthier men and a healthier world. Movember is our time to unite. To take on mental health, suicide, prostate cancer and testicular cancer. And you coming along for the ride only makes us stronger. The Mo is calling. How will you answer?

Movember is our month-long fundraising and awareness campaign starting November 1st. During Movember, men sign up to Grow a moustache for 30 days, getting friends, family and colleagues to donate to their effort. Men and women can also get involved by committing to Move and walking or running 60km over the month, or by hosting an event – bringing together friends to change the face of men’s health.
